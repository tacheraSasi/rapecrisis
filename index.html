Who We Are

Empowering Survivors with Compassionate Support

Rape Crisis Tanzania is dedicated to providing survivors of sexual violence with the support and resources they need to heal and seek justice.

  • We provide confidential counseling services.
  • Our legal team offers advocacy and representation.
  • We advocate for policy changes to support survivors' rights.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Rape Crisis offer?

Rape Crisis provides support and services to survivors of sexual violence, including crisis intervention, counseling, and advocacy.

How can I volunteer with Rape Crisis?

Volunteers play a crucial role in supporting our mission. Visit our volunteer page to learn more about opportunities and how to get involved.

What should I do if I or someone I know has experienced sexual violence?

If you or someone you know has experienced sexual violence, you can contact our helpline for immediate support and guidance on next steps.

Does Rape Crisis offer educational programs?

Rape Crisis conducts educational workshops and outreach programs aimed at raising awareness about sexual violence prevention and consent.

How can I support the work of Rape Crisis?

You can support our work by donating, volunteering, spreading awareness, or collaborating with us on advocacy efforts.

Where can I find more information about Rape Crisis?

For more information about Rape Crisis, our services, and how you can get involved, please visit our website or contact us directly.
